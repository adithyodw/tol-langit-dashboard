## Risk Disclosures

**Trading foreign exchange, gold (XAU/USD), and other leveraged instruments involves substantial risk of loss and may not be suitable for all investors.** The use of financial leverage amplifies both gains and losses and can result in losses exceeding your initial deposit.

- **Past performance is not indicative of future results.** All returns shown are historical, verified, and drawn from real-money live accounts. They do not constitute a guarantee or projection of future performance.
- **Equity drawdown vs. balance drawdown are distinct risk measures.** Strategies employing grid mechanics (V10, V10 HR) may sustain periods of extreme floating (unrealised) equity drawdown before positions are closed at a profit. Investors must assess equity drawdown — not solely balance drawdown — when evaluating these strategies.
- **Grid and position-recovery mechanics structurally amplify risk.** A high historical win rate in grid-based strategies is a mechanical consequence of deferred losses, not evidence of low risk.
- **The ETF strategy is under active drawdown recovery.** Capital preservation, not return generation, is the current priority for TLETF. Allocation is not recommended until balance drawdown recovers materially below 30%.
- **TLETFG and TLETF have insufficient track records** (2 months and 4 months respectively) to constitute statistically meaningful performance characterisation. Formal allocation to these strategies carries heightened model risk.
